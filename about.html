About Sendly

Sendly exists to support organisations that regularly handle document-based communication and dispatch as part of their administrative or operational workflows.

For many institutions, activities such as printing, folding, addressing, and preparing documents for dispatch consume significant staff time and operational effort, despite not being core business functions.

Reducing Operational Costs

Managing document preparation in-house often requires maintaining printing infrastructure, allocating manpower, and handling peak workload variations.

Sendly helps reduce these costs by offering structured, activity-based support services that eliminate the need for:

  • Dedicated in-house printing infrastructure
  • Temporary or ad-hoc manpower for dispatch preparation
  • Repeated setup effort for recurring document formats
  • Inefficiencies caused by fragmented execution

Clients benefit from predictable, requirement-based costs aligned with actual work performed.

Improving Productivity

Administrative and operations teams are often required to divert time and attention to manual preparation tasks during dispatch cycles.

By outsourcing document preparation and pre-mailing activities to Sendly, organisations are able to:

  • Allow staff to focus on approvals and core responsibilities
  • Reduce internal handovers and coordination effort
  • Manage peak dispatch volumes without disruption
  • Ensure consistency across large document batches

This results in improved turnaround times and smoother internal workflows.

Where Sendly Fits

Sendly operates as a private service provider offering printing and pre-mailing support services.

Our role is limited to preparation, execution, and dispatch readiness. Postal booking, delivery, and tracking are governed by applicable postal regulations and handled by the respective postal service provider.
