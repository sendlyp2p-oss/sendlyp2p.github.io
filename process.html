Our Process

Sendly follows a structured, step-based approach to ensure clarity, accuracy, and readiness at every stage of document preparation.

Each stage is executed with defined responsibility boundaries and controlled handling of client information.

Step 1: Requirement Confirmation

We begin by understanding the scope of work, document type, volume, timelines, and preparation requirements.

  • Confirmation of document category and volume
  • Understanding of preparation and personalisation needs
  • Clarification of timelines and delivery expectations

Step 2: File Review & Preparation

Client-provided files and data are reviewed for print readiness, formatting consistency, and preparation feasibility.

  • Format and layout review
  • Basic proofreading and consistency checks
  • Address formatting and pincode reference checks

Step 3: Design, Editing & Data Handling

Where required, documents are structured and data is prepared for variable printing and personalisation.

  • Layout structuring and template standardisation
  • Variable data placement and verification
  • Controlled handling of sensitive information

Step 4: Printing & Pre-Mailing Execution

Approved documents are printed and prepared through structured pre-mailing activities.

  • Batch-wise printing execution
  • Folding, insertion, and envelope preparation
  • Sorting and bundling as per requirement

Step 5: Dispatch Readiness & Confirmation

Completed articles are verified and made ready for submission through standard postal channels.

  • Batch verification before handover
  • Internal readiness checks
  • Confirmation of completion to the client

Postal booking, acceptance, delivery, and tracking are governed by applicable postal regulations and service conditions.
