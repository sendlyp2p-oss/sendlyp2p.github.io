Postal Tariff – Reference Only

The postal tariff information provided below is shared strictly for reference and planning purposes, based on publicly available postal tariff data for domestic document mail.

Actual charges applicable at the time of booking shall prevail and are governed by the applicable postal regulations and notifications.

Speed Post – Document Tariff (Domestic)

The table below indicates Speed Post charges for documents based on weight and distance slabs.

Weight / Distance Local Up to 200 km 201 – 500 km 501 – 1000 km 1001 – 2000 km Above 2000 km
Up to 50 grams ₹19 ₹47 ₹47 ₹47 ₹47 ₹47
51 – 250 grams ₹24 ₹59 ₹63 ₹68 ₹72 ₹77
251 – 500 grams ₹28 ₹70 ₹75 ₹82 ₹86 ₹93

GST: Applicable as per prevailing statutory provisions.

Speed Post – Value Added Services (Documents)

  • Registration: ₹5 per article (GST extra, as applicable)
  • OTP-based delivery: ₹5 per article (GST extra, as applicable)
  • Proof of Delivery (POD): ₹10 per article

Ordinary Letter – Document Tariff (Untracked)

Ordinary letters are non-trackable and generally used for routine communication.

  • First 20 grams: ₹5
  • Each additional 20 grams or part thereof: ₹5
  • Applicable uniformly across India

GST is applicable on letters exceeding the prescribed weight limits, as per prevailing regulations.

Important Notes

  • Tariffs are subject to revision without prior notice
  • Actual charges are determined at the time of postal booking
  • Service availability may vary by location
  • Postal tariffs are independent of Sendly’s service charges

Clients are advised to refer to official postal department publications for the most current and authoritative tariff information.
