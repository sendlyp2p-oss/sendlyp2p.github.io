Our Process

A structured, execution-focused workflow designed to support institutions with reliable print and pre-mailing operations.

1. Requirement Confirmation

We begin by understanding document volumes, timelines, formats, and institutional requirements to clearly define the scope of execution.

2. File Review & Preparation

Client-provided files are reviewed for print readiness, formatting accuracy, and batch consistency to minimise execution errors.

3. Printing & Pre-Mailing Execution

Documents are printed, folded or flattened, inserted, and grouped according to the agreed preparation structure.

4. Batch Verification

Prepared batches are verified for quantity, sequencing, and consistency before being marked ready for dispatch.

5. Dispatch Readiness & Handover

Finalised batches are handed over in a condition suitable for submission through standard postal channels, as per agreed coordination timelines.

Scope Clarity

Sendly’s role is limited to document preparation, execution, and dispatch readiness. Postal booking, delivery, and tracking are governed by applicable postal regulations and handled by the respective postal service provider.
